10 Years with ONCE

In 2015, TWICE took their first step into the world with the greeting, "One in a million, TWICE!" With generation-defining hits like 'CHEER UP', 'TT', 'What is Love?', and 'Feel Special', they have captivated audiences far beyond K-POP, reaching the global stage.

From the sweat and tears behind the scenes to the dazzling spotlights of their world tours, this documentary reveals the genuine stories of their growth, unbreakable bond, and visions for the future. Brighter than the stage lights were the past 10 years, and at the center of it all was always their fandom, 'ONCE'.

Celebrating their 10th anniversary, [ONE IN A MILL10N] unfolds on the big screen, capturing the true essence of the one and only TWICE.
